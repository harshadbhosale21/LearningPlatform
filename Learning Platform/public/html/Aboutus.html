About iEducate

EdIndia is a section 8 Not for Profit Company that leverages technology and data analytics to create innovative solutions to impact the quality of education at scale. It has been provided incubation support by Sterlite Power Transmission and its group companies. we are dedicated to fostering a lifelong love for learning. We believe that education is the key to personal growth, empowerment, and a brighter future. Our mission is to provide accessible and engaging educational resources for learners of all ages and backgrounds.

Our Work

Acknowledging the fact that there are existing barriers to integrate technology within Government system; EdIndia spearheads initiatives that capitalize on modern digital tools, smart classrooms as well as training of teachers in facilitating children to participate better in the education process and realize improved learning outcomes. EdIndia has been running projects in intersection of Education, Technology and Data Analytics in 100 schools in Rajasthan and 1500 Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai (MCGM) schools in Mumbai, Maharashtra, since 2012. These programs impact more than 3 lakh students

Our Focus

Identify Needs

Identify needs for better learning outcomes based on an analysis of secondary data collected periodically by the education system. It provides decision makers with relevant dashboards for informed decision making with the focus on academic trends and patterns.

Levarage Technology

Leverage technology to build teacher's facilitation skills to engage students, plan teaching sessions and leverage learning aids for actual and demonstrated improvement in the learning environment

Teacher Support

Develop a repository of 'Mobile First' digital tools with content focused on 'How to do' in vernacular to support teachers to plan and transact teaching plans for engaging and interactive teaching learning process.
